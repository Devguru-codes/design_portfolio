AI Money Mentor (DhanSarthi)

An AI-Powered Finance Assistant

Product Overview & Positioning

AI Money Mentor is a proposed AI-driven personal finance assistant app. It leverages a multi-agent architecture to give everyday Indians affordable, on-demand financial advice. The system orchestrates specialized AI agents for tasks like tax planning, investment analysis, and budgeting.

Unlike traditional banking apps that only show data, this product offers friendly AI guidance across multiple domains. It is targeted at mid-market consumers (e.g. salaried professionals, young families, gig workers) who may have some investible income but no financial planner.

Problem Statement

750+ million employed Indians lack affordable personal finance support. Users face confusing choices and often feel anxious about money. Traditional apps present raw data but don't guide users in plain language. Furthermore, users mistrust automated advice (63% report chatbot security concerns). Our product must solve these pain points by providing personalized, explainable financial recommendations in a conversational UI.

Why AI for Personal Finance?

AI automates expertise and personalizes advice at scale. It can learn a user’s specific goals and craft relevant suggestions. It auto-categorizes expenses, reminds users about bill payments, and enables 24/7 interaction, shifting from a reactive platform to a proactive financial partner.

Conversational UX & Trust

Designing a finance AI requires careful attention to trust and transparency:

  • Clarity & Simplicity: Natural, jargon-free language.
  • User Control: Quick suggestion buttons and data usage toggles.
  • Trust Through Branding: "Powered by Money Mentor AI" with verified logos.
  • Proactive Engagement: Nudging users intelligently (e.g. "I noticed you spent 20% more on dining").
  • Explainable AI: Providing "Why this?" reasoning and citing data sources.

Information Architecture

The app’s structure organizes content into core areas:

  • Dashboard: Overview of net worth, budgets, upcoming bills.
  • AI Chat Assistant ("Mentor"): The heart of the app, accessible everywhere.
  • Wallet/Accounts: View-only linked bank and investment accounts.
  • Goals: Set and track financial goals.
  • Insights: Analytical dashboards with charts.

UX Layouts & Visuals

Dashboard: Multi-section layout. "Net Worth" card spans full width. Below, Budget and Bills side-by-side. Clean, grid-aligned layout with consistent margins.

Chat UI: Full-screen vertical flow. Bot messages have a soft blue background. Suggested quick-reply chips appear above the input field.

Color Scheme: A trustworthy palette of blues and teals (#2196F3, #4CAF50). White backgrounds with charcoal text. A high-contrast dark theme is also available.

Metrics & KPIs

To evaluate the success of the AI Money Mentor, we track:

  • User Engagement: Chat Sessions per user, DAU/MAU ratio.
  • Goal Completion Rate: % of users meeting targets.
  • Advice Adoption: % of suggested actions taken by users.
  • NPS: Trust and confidence scores.
